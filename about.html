-Our Story-

The legacy of NZ Fashion dates back to the early 1950s when Mr. Robot and his family conceived the idea of establishing a fashion brand catering to local customers. In 1952, their vision materialized as a small, home-based clothing shop specializing in women's apparel. This innovative business endeavor revolutionized the local market by providing affordable access to high-quality, fashionable garments.

Our comprehensive collection encompasses a wide range of attire, including frocks, abayas, shalwars, tops, jeans, shalwar materials, shawls, and children's clothing. As a fashion brand, we prioritize delivering cutting-edge and stylish clothing while maintaining uncompromising quality.

For over five decades, NZ Fashion has operated as a traditional family business, with all management and operational aspects overseen by family members. In line with evolving industry trends, our pivotal milestone was the inauguration of our first flagship NZ Fashion store in Kandy in 2004. Despite facing formidable competition from industry giants and renowned fashion brands, our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction has served as the driving force behind our perseverance.

Presently, we proudly own and operate two well-regarded NZ Fashion stores, which have garnered an exceptional reputation not only for their apparel and fashion offerings but also for their exemplary customer service. This remarkable achievement is a testament to the unwavering support of our esteemed clientele.
