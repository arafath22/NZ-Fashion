How to create an account in your website?
To create a user account in NZ Fashion website, follow the below steps,
  1. Go to our Home page by clicking home buttom in the navigation bar
  2. Scroll down to the Sign-in section
  3. Click on Sign-up now link, the link will take you to the login page
  4. Fill-out the form with relevant information and click on Create Account button
  5. Once done, the Home page will be viewed. Scroll down to Sign-in section again
  6. Enter your Username and the Password in the fields and click on Log-in button
Or,
  1. Scroll down to the footer
  2. CLick on Create an account option under QUICK LINKS
  3. Fill-out the form with relevant information and click on Create Account button
  4. Once done, the Home page will be viewed. Scroll down to Sign-in section again
  5. Enter your Username and the Password in the fields and click on Log-in button
For further technical assistance, feel free to contact our technical support team through +94 77 777 7775
